About Me


My name is August Bottiger, a student currently attending Penn State University. I'm pursuing a degree in Information Technology for Application Development. I have an eye for detail and a mind suited to deal with complicated logic, and I wish to become proficient enough at coding to make a career out of it.


Throughout my time in high school and college, I have learned quite a few things about IST, including the following topics; database management, the Java programming language, HTML and CSS design, and Security and Risk Analysis. I prefer to work by myself without being interrupted, although I am capable of working with a team and collaborate. I thrive in environments that require problem-solving and detail-oriented thinking.


On my personal time, I often research topics I'm heavily interested in and try to learn as much as I can about it. While I've rarely participated in extracurricular activities regarding programming, I would gladly start attending these activities as long as the environment is not hostile towards my senses. I wish to become adept at programming, to the point where I don't need to reference other sources unless it's a topic I'm not familiar with.


I'm driven by an urge to solve problems of all forms, ranging from technological to even emotional or social. I love the idea of being able to have a career in a topic I'm genuinely interested in, essentially being paid to do something I enjoy doing instead of menial work.




Thank you for visiting my website. Feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out if you'd like to connect or collaborate on any projects.
