About Me



Call me Jonah! I'm a third-year computer science student looking to make a career in the field of cybersecurity. I was born in 2002, attended Liberty Highschool, and went to my first two years of secondary education at Northampton Area Community College before transferring to Penn State Lehigh Valley.



My passion is for manipulating abstractions. Every problem is a puzzle, and the world of programming and mathematics abounds with opportunities for elegant solutions. When I'm not crunching for classes, I enjoy working on personal programming projects or woodworking. I've always loved videogames, and I aspire to put together and release a project as a hobbyist someday. I have some experience in making digital assets like textures and 3D models from my days as a young Minecraft texture pack maker. I even made a little money in commissions for my work. I dabble in a few other hobbies as well. I've tried longsword fencing, and play tabletop RPGs when I have time. I also have an overriding fascination with philosophy, and the ways in which it can be expressed across all manner of disciplines.
